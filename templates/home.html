How to Play

1. Someone Creates a Poll

A topic with exactly three options. For example: "Most popular Muppet" with Kermit, Fozzie, and Animal.

2. Everyone Votes Strategically

Don't vote for what you think will be first. Vote for what you think will come second.

3. Winners Are Revealed

Everyone who voted for the second-place option wins. It's all about reading the room.
